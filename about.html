About Livsma

A group of indivuiduals on a mission to change the definition of technology. A motivation obtained through different sources to make our vision reality. We as a team are always up for an idea to solve the problem around us. From an application to a product, we do not restrict our path to find a solution. We as a team might not be the best, but we are trying get better everyday.

Mission and Vision

Mission - To provide a platform for people to connect, share, discuss and help each other being a human irrespective of our race, caste or birth.

Vision - To build a society which is ready to connect, help and adopt our modern technologies to help build a better place for the upcoming generations to live and grow happily.
